BRANDING WEBSITE PACKAGING

OnHand is a medical glove company that needed a logo, packaging designs and a website. Their aim was to be modern, minimal and clean.

Made at Mirk

Made at Mirk

01

Packaging Design

I constructed the logo and colour scheme to match similar medical companies to include hand/care imagery. I then applied it to the products they had designed. Their priority was that it was easy to recognise for use of medical professionals.

02

Website Design + Functionality

For the web design, I continued a design done by the lead designer at Mirk, implementing the client's changes and adjustments. I converted this file to invision to share with the developers to see if they had any queries.

From there I created a responsive mockup in Webflow for the client to review and test. I then implemented animations and transitions plus full interactivity with the panels and videos provided.
