ILLUSTRATION DESIGN BRANDING

For my Twitch channel, I developed emotes, badges and screens to match my new brand direction.

As Twitch users prefer dark mode, I chose to make a dark themed layout with my icons and status bars. The emotes and badges stand out better as lighter colours and are for the enjoyment of the viewers, so I did not have to be as precise with my branding.
